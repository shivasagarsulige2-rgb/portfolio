Welcome

Hi, I’m Shiva Sagar — a data science student at Keuka College, passionate about turning complex data into clear, actionable insights. Beyond the classroom, I lead student organisations, advocate for accessibility, and volunteer across my community.

This portfolio showcases my technical projects, academic journey, leadership experience, and the skills I’ve developed throughout my studies.

Portfolio Sections

Academic Journey Data Science student at Keuka College since 2021. Specialising in machine learning, NLP, and reproducible research with Python and Quarto.

Research & Projects Built interactive data dashboards, compared BERT vs T5 for sentiment analysis, and benchmarked classification models — all with live, executable Python code.

Leadership & Service President of the Science Club, Treasurer of DACE, and President of the Campus Activities Board. Volunteer with animal rescues, the Red Cross, and youth leadership programmes.
