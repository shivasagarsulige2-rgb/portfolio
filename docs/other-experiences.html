Reflection

NoteGrowth Through Real Experience

Starting and running Wrench Mate taught me something no course could fully replicate: decision-making under uncertainty with real consequences. These experiences now inform how I approach analytics — not as an academic exercise, but as a practical tool for solving genuine business problems.
